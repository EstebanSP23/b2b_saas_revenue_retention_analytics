\## Assumptions (MVP)

\- 36 months of monthly data

\- 10,000 customers acquired over time (increasing with noise)

\- Plans: Basic ($50), Plus ($100), Pro ($200)

\- Monthly churn: 3% of active customers

\- Monthly plan changes: 7% of active customers (70% upgrades / 30% downgrades)

\- No reactivation in MVP
